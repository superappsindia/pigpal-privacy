About PigPal

Your trusted piggery management companion.

Our Mission

PigPal is designed to help pig farmers, smallholders, and agribusinesses manage their farms more efficiently. From breeding logs to feed tracking, PigPal reduces paperwork, improves accuracy, and saves time.

Key Features

  • 🐖 Pig List — track each pig by tag, gender, and stage of growth.
  • ❤️ Breeding logs, heat cycles, and family tree records.
  • 💉 Vaccination & Deworming reminders.
  • ⚖️ Weight estimation & FCR monitoring.
  • 📦 Feed and inventory management.
  • 💰 Income & Expense tracking.
  • 🔔 Smart task reminders for today and tomorrow.

Who We Are
